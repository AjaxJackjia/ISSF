Invited Speaker

Dr. Jia He (何佳)

现任南方科技大学领军教授, 教育部及李嘉诚基金会“长江学者讲座教授“，清华大学双聘教授及博导，上海交通大学教授及博导，四川大学教授及博导, 深交所博士后导师，中国金融学会常务理事及学术委员会委员， 武汉市政府金融顾问，成都市政府金融咨询委员会委员, 泉州市政府金融顾问；担任多家境内外企业独立董事，包括中投证券， 清华同方股份，中国银河国际金控，中国诚通发展集团，东英投资等。 何教授在加入南方科技大学前，长期任教于香港中文大学(工学院副教授（1996-1998）, 商学院教授(1998-2005))， 于2000年创办及主持（2000-2015）在金融业内有着很大影响的香港中文大学-清华大学金融方向MBA项目，并且长期担任香港中文大学中国金融改革发展研究中心主任，在此期间受21世纪经济报道主持每年一次的在业内有很大影响力的亚洲银行竞争力研究（2006-2015）)。

何教授曾经担任过中国证券监督管理委员会规划发展委员会委员，深圳证券交易所综合研究所所长，深圳证券交易所博士後工作站学术总指导，他的政策研究成果在中国金融业界和监管部门得到很高的评价（其研究成果被周小川主席批转到全国各个交易所和监管局，被“新华文摘”全文转载， 被2002年一篇轰动全国的文章“深圳， 谁抛弃了你”高度评价, 凤凰卫视10来此专访以及彭博和21世纪经济报道多次采访）。

何佳教授还担任过上海交通大学金融系主任，中欧国际工商管理学院EMBA核心教授，中国香港特区政府大学研究基金评审委成员（RGC Panel member），美国休斯顿大学终生教授, 美国宾西凡尼亚大学LINK中心（诺奖获得者克莱因教授创立并主持）的研究助理， 还在地质部150工程从事石油勘探数据处理（1978-1980）；在他所有的经历里，他最引以自豪的经历是在中国人民解放军黑龙江生产建社兵团6师61团屯垦戍边（1971-1975），当年他在那里的经历曾经被上海人民广播电台和光明日报报道过， 并在1975年被推荐去黑龙江大学成为一名工农兵学员。

1988年何佳在美国宾西凡尼亚大学沃顿商学院获得金融财务博士学位，他的论文发表在Journal of Finance, Journal of Business, Journal of International Business Studies, Journal of International Money and Finance, Journal of Business and Economic Statistics, Journal of Corporate Finance and Economic Letters.，其研究成果被主要学术期刊及学术专著引用：例如American Economic Review, Journal of Accounting Economics, Journal of Business, Journal of Business and Economic Statistics, Journal of Econometrics, Journal of Finance, Journal of Financial Economics, Journal of Financial and Quantitative Analysis, Journal of International Business Studies, Review of Economics and Statistics, Review of Financial Studies, University Chicago Law Review; The Econometrics of Financial Markets(Campbell, J., A. Lo, and A. MacKinlay, 1997, Princeton University Press), Credit Risk, Princeton University Press(Duffie, D. and K. Singleton, 2003)，Empirical Dynamic Asset Pricing (Singleton, K. 2006, Princeton University Press)

He currently is the Leading Professor at South University of Science and Technology of China as well as the Chair Professor in Chang Jiang Scholars Program. He also severs as the Professor in Tsinghua University, Shanghai Jiao Tong University and Sichuan University. He is also the consultant of several local governments and departments. He also severs as independent director in some corporations, like China Investment Securities, Tsinghua Tongfang, China Galaxy Financial Holdings Company Limited and China Chengtong Development Group Limited.

Prior to joining SUSTC, he was the professor in the Chinese University of Hong Kong (Vice Prof in Faculty of Engineering (1996-1998), Prof in Faculty of Business Administration (1998-2015)) and he held the MBA in Finance program by Tsinghua University and the Chinese Univ. of HK. He also worked as the director of Center for the Chinese Financial Development and Reform in Chinese Univ. of HK and host the Asian Banks Competitiveness Research annually invited by the 21st Century Business Herald. He also served as Commissioner at the Planning and Development Committee of China Securities Regulatory Commission and director at the Research Institute of Shenzhen Stock Exchange.

His accomplishments in policy research received highly assessment and were forwarded to both financial industry and regulatory commission by Zhou Xiaochuan, the President of People’s Bank of China. In the meantime, he also served as the director of Finance department in Shanghai Jiao Tong University, professor in EMBA at China Europe International Business School, RGC Panel member, tenure-track professor at University of Houston, and research assistant in Link Project led by L.R.Klein who is the 1980 Nobel economics prize laureate and technician in Project 150 of Ministry of Land and Resources （1978-1980）. Among all his experience, the one that he is most proud of is the period (1971-1975) that he served as a solider in PLA in Heilongjiang province, which was reported by media and newspaper, and he was recommended to be a WPSS in Heilongjiang University after that.

Lecture theme演讲主题:

中国金融稳定发展研究

简介: 30年来中国金融体系发展迅速，但是也面临巨大问题。金融稳定已经成了国家宏观政策的主要目标之一。我们用金融学基本原理分析中国金融体系的根本问题，进而找出导致中国金融不稳定的主要因素和政府干预的基本逻辑。
