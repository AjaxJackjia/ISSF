Invited Speaker

Yihan Fang (方以涵)

CEO of Yirendai

Yihan Fang is the CEO of Yirendai, a leading online lending marketplace in China. She has 15 years of experience in internet and financial services.

Prior to joining Yirendai, Ms. Fang served as director of marketing products at Nelnet/CUNet in 2011, a leading provider of digital enrollment marketing solutions. Ms. Fang worked at IAC/Ask.com in multiple positions from 2002 to 2010, including Vice President of Global Search and Answers overseeing strategy and product development of various key search and question/answer products.

Yihan received a Ph.M. and M.S. in electrical engineering and a M.A. in astronomy from Columbia University. She completed her undergraduate study through the Program for Gifted Youth at the University of Science and Technology of China.

方以涵，宜信宜人贷总经理。加入宜信之前，方以涵拥有十几年在美国互联网行业专业经验，曾在美国上市公司IAC/Ask.com任副总裁，及CUnet任市场产品总监。方以涵于2011年进入宜信，并于2012年牵头创办宜信互联网部，主要负责管理宜信旗下个人对个人信用借款与理财咨询服务平台宜人贷。
