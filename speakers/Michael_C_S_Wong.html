Invited Speaker

Dr. Michael C S Wong (王泽森)

Dr. Michael C S Wong is an Associate Professor of Finance at City University of Hong Kong and an expert in Asia on bank risk management under the Basel II/III framework. He architected/reviewed risk management systems and models for more than 30 banks, submitted independent risk assessment reports to company boards and regulators, and provided expert witness on legal disputes involving complex financial transactions. Dr. Wong served as a founding member of Global Association of Risk Professionals’ FRM Committee, an examiner of professional examinations organized respectively by HKSI and HKIB, a member of the Advisory Board of China Bond Rating in Beijing, a non-executive director of Bank of Communications Trustee, and an appointed member of Market Misconduct Tribunal in Hong Kong. He published more than 40 academic journal articles, 10 book chapters and 5 professional books.

王泽森教授是香港城市大学金融学副教授，是巴塞尔协议银行监管标准的亚洲专家。他帮助超过30亚洲 家银行设计或审查风险管理系统和风险模型，向金融机构董事会和金融监管机构提交独立风险评估报告，及为金融交易纠纷提供法律专家证人的独立意见。王教授曾任全球风险专业人士协会的 FRM 委员会创立成员，香港证券学院和香港银行学会专业考试委员会成员、北京中债资信有限公司顾问委员会成员、交通银行信托有限公司非执行董事， 香港市场失当行为审裁处委任成员。他出版了超过 50篇 学术文章和5 本专业书籍。
