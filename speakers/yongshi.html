Invited Speaker

Dr. Yong Shi (石勇)

Yong Shi serves as the Director, Chinese Academy of Sciences Research Center on Fictitious Economy & Data Science and the Director of the Key Lab of Big Data Mining and Knowledge Management, Chinese Academy of Sciences. His research interests include business intelligence, data mining, and multiple criteria decision making. He has published more than 24 books, over 300 papers in various journals and numerous conferences/proceedings papers. He is the Editor-in-Chief of International Journal of Information Technology and Decision Making (SCI), Editor-in-Chief of Annals of Data Science (Springer) and a member of Editorial Board for a number of academic journals. Dr. Shi has received many distinguished awards including the Fellow of the World Academy of Sciences for developing countries, 2015, the Georg Cantor Award of the International Society on Multiple Criteria Decision Making (MCDM), 2009; Fudan Prize of Distinguished Contribution in Management, Fudan Premium Fund of Management, China, 2009; Outstanding Young Scientist Award, National Natural Science Foundation of China, 2001; and Speaker of Distinguished Visitors Program (DVP) for 1997-2000, IEEE Computer Society.

演讲题目：

Big Data Analytics and Credit Scoring

演讲概要：

Big Data has become a reality that no one can ignore. Big Data shows a big opportunity for academia and industry. This talk first presents the cutting-edge concepts and techniques in Big Data analytics, which includes the challenges of Big Data analytics as well as Data Science, the scientific issues behind Big Data. Then, this talk discusses Credit Scoring as an important Big Data application and elaborates the small and micro merchant credit scoring model, based on millions of china merchants’ daily and real time business data. The model effectively eases the loan problem of small and micro enterprises in China, and has been successfully used in China Everbright Bank and other financial institutions.
