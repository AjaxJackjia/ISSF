Competition

The subject of this contest is strategy development of quantitative investment. This contest aims to discover and cultivate top-level talents who are into quantitative investment and to promote the development of “Smart Finance”. The contest committee is composed of famous domestic and international scholars and practitioners who are renowned for quantitative investment. They will provide guidance, trainings and Q&As through out the contest. The contest will base on the quantitative investment terminal of Quantrader (国泰安) for researching, editing and testing of strategies. The contestants will use historical data to conduct re-test, submit strategy model & paper. The contestants will also be provided with an account of CSMAR (the listed company finance session, the listed company trade session and the information session), a world famous finance database to assist them to conduct any data analysis. The contest committee will score and rank each contestant/team. The contest will be carried out online, and no physical competition area will be required.

1. Time scale

  • Application: 4.30 - 5.30
  • Opening ceremony: 5.21
  • Contest: 5.30 - 8.20
  • Award ceremony (provisional): 9.23

2. Target Applicants

The contest is open to undergraduates, postgraduates and PhDs from Mainland China, Hong Kong, Taiwan and America etc. who are interested in Smart Finance. The applicants are not required to specialize in any particular major. However, finance, mathematics, information and computer science etc. are recommended.

3. Team Requirements

This is a team competition with 2-3 persons for each team. Contestants from different universities or majors are allowed to form a team. However, it is recommended that each team shall include a contestant majored in finance, mathematics or computer-related areas. Each team will be provided with an account number.

4. Target Applicants

The application fee is RMB 1,500. USD, HKD or TWD will be exchanged on the prevailing date.

5. How to apply

Applicants shall register at Quantrader, on successful submission of an account, go to the official website of Smart Finance to make the application, fill the relevant information and pay the application fee.

6. Team Guidance and Collaborations

To enhance guidance and collaborations among teams and/or team members, all contestants may communicate via IdeaWorks.

All teams need to register and establish a team project at IdeaWorks and nominate a team leader. In the meantime, each team shall invite a tutor to supervise its work. The tutor can be a teacher from any team member’s university. Team members shall communicate via the platform on issues arising from the establishment of models, under the guidance of the tutor and the leadership of the team leader.

In addition, the contest will have managers, consisting of volunteers. These managers, after being trained by the contest committee, will answer enquiries from teams and provide training where necessary. To be specific, 15 team leaders and 1 manager will form a project. The manager will communicate with each team leader within the project.
